Eric W. Mcinroy said he holds a 5.4% stake in Transmation Inc.'s common shares outstanding.
In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Mr. Mcinroy, president of Transcat Inc., a subsidiary of Transmation, said he holds 124,875 Transmation common shares, including 8,000 shares purchased on Sept. 6 for $30,000.
Eric Mcinroy said his 5.4% stake in Rochester, N.Y.-based Transmation is ``strictly an investment.
