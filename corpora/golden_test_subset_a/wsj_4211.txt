Utilicorp United said it is seeking regulatory approval for a public offering of two million common shares.
The utility said proceeds from the sale would be used to reduce short-term debt and for general corporate purposes. An underwriter hasn't been selected, Utilicorp said.
Utilicorp said the sale would increase its common shares outstanding to 22.8 million shares. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Utilicorp shares closed at $19, down 37.5 cents.
